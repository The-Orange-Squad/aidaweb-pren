Welcome to AIDA Web Prompt Engineering

A platform for creating, managing, and exploring creative prompts.

This platform is designed for AIDA Web users and members of The Orange Squad who are looking to find or share prompts for the AIDA chatbot. These prompts can be used in the chat or run as custom instructions. Whether you're looking to enhance your interactions with AIDA or contribute your own unique prompts, you've come to the right place.
